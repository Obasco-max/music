Mavo

Mavo is a 21-year-old Nigerian artist known for his "Burbur Music," a blend of Afrobeats, rap, and Nigerian slang. Born Marvin Oseremen Ukanigbe, he balances his fast-rising music career with his studies in optometry at Afe Babalola University. He gained widespread recognition after the viral hit "Escaladizzy" and has since released projects like Ukanigbe and SANKO, collaborating with artists such as Davido, Ayra Starr, and Wizkid.
